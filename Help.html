User account Support

  • When you loginto your user Account you can see your details.if you have any requrement to change your details you can use Edit details button in leftside corner.but you are restirted to edit some details of you.if you want to update details completely you can contact a admin.

  • Then you can use Apply leave button if you required to get a leave.you will get a small form to fill there and you can fill that and submit the leave application.It will redirect to the executive and he accept or decline that.

  • when you apply a leave it has to be accpeted by an executive so you can see wether he has accepted it or decline it through View your leave applications button

  • You can see what are task you have assinged already by clicking view your tasks button

Executive side support

  • When you login to Executive login you can see all the Employees who has registered to the system with their details.you have an option called view for view employee details in a profile of them.

  • You have another option called assign tasks. If you want any employee to assign a work in the organization you can click that option then you will get a samll form. you can fill it and assign them for any work.They can review that Task in their profile.

  • You have the permission for accept or decline leave requsets from employees.so for do that you can click and watch all the leave application by clicking View leave requests button. in there you will have two options called accept or decline you can click and give them permission.Updated leave application will be there in employee's profile.

Admin support

  • As an admin you can see all the users in the Systems, Users as well as Executives. you have All view,Edit and Delete users and Executive Options.you can executive these CRUD operations by clicking View button,Edit button,Delete button
  • If the organization wants add new Executive profile for new executive you can do that by clicking Add new Executive button. You will get a signup page when you click that you can then register that executive profile.
